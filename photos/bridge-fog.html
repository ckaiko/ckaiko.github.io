Morning Fog Over the Bridge

San Francisco, CA · October 2025

The Story

I woke up at 5:30 AM specifically for this shot. The fog in San Francisco is never guaranteed, but when it rolls in and wraps around the bridge towers, it's absolutely worth the early alarm. Stood on Battery Spencer for about an hour waiting for the light to break through.

Camera Details

Fujifilm X-T4 56mm f/1.2 ISO 200 1/500s

Notes

Minimal editing on this one — the fog did most of the work. Just a slight curves adjustment and a touch of clarity to bring out the bridge's silhouette.
