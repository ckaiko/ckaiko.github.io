Golden Hour at the Old Train Station

Portland, OR · December 2025

The Story

I stumbled onto this station while wandering around Portland's Pearl District right at sunset. The light bent through the old iron arches in a way that felt almost cinematic. I had about five minutes before the angle changed — sometimes the best shots are the ones you barely catch.

Camera Details

Fujifilm X-T4 35mm f/1.4 ISO 400 1/250s

Notes

Edited in Lightroom with a slightly warm white balance and lifted shadows. The original had more contrast but I wanted it to feel soft and nostalgic.
