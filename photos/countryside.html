Road Trip Through the Countryside

Blue Ridge Parkway, NC · April 2025

The Story

Three days, 300 miles of the Blue Ridge Parkway. We pulled over every time the view changed — which was roughly every five minutes. This particular shot was taken at an overlook around milepost 350. The clouds were sitting right at eye level and the road just disappeared into them.

Camera Details

Fujifilm X-T4 16mm f/2.8 ISO 200 1/1000s

Notes

Wide-angle to capture the scale of the mountains. Boosted the greens slightly to match how vivid everything looked in person. Spring in the Appalachians is stunning.
