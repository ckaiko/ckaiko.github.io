Web Dev & Data Science Research Apprentice

September – December 2023 · CoolClimate Network · Berkeley, CA

Role Overview

Worked as a research apprentice at UC Berkeley's CoolClimate Network, combining web development and data science to help local governments understand and reduce greenhouse gas emissions. My work ranged from pulling census data via API to designing interactive visualizations for city planners.

Key Responsibilities

  • Built API requests to pull census and demographic data for greenhouse gas emission analysis across U.S. communities
  • Cleaned, transformed, and aggregated large datasets to produce accurate emission estimates at the city and county level
  • Created interactive data visualizations and charts to communicate findings to non-technical stakeholders
  • Contributed to the design and front-end development of the CoolClimate website, improving usability for city planners and policymakers
