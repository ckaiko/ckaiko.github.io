About Me

A little bit about who I am

Hi, I'm Cecilia Aiko — a project manager and developer based in the Pacific Northwest. I love building things that live at the intersection of clean code and great teamwork.

By day I lead cross-functional engineering squads, run sprints, and keep roadmaps on track. By night (and most weekends) I'm shipping side projects, experimenting with new tools, and collecting vintage things I definitely don't need.

I'm passionate about developer experience, thoughtful design, and making the complex feel simple. When I'm not at a screen you'll find me thrifting, exploring record shops, or photographing whatever catches my eye.
