Budget Buddy

A personal finance tracker built with SwiftUI

Swift SwiftUI CoreData

Overview

Budget Buddy is an iOS app for tracking personal expenses. It detects recurring charges, generates monthly summaries with category breakdowns, and lets you export everything as CSV for spreadsheet lovers.

I built it as a learning project to get comfortable with SwiftUI and CoreData. It's currently my daily driver for expense tracking.

Key Features

  • Recurring expense auto-detection
  • Monthly and yearly summary views with pie charts
  • Category tagging with custom colors
  • CSV export for use in spreadsheets
  • iCloud sync across devices

What I Learned

SwiftUI's declarative style was a big mental shift coming from React. I gained a solid understanding of CoreData relationships, iCloud sync configuration, and Apple's Human Interface Guidelines.
